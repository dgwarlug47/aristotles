Ἅμαρτια • Character Analysis

In the tradition of Aristotelian poetics, we examine the souls of great characters through their tragic flaws—their hamartia. Each figure is studied for the fatal weakness that shapes their destiny, the context wherein this flaw wreaks its greatest havoc, and their measure of phronesis—that practical wisdom which separates the wise from the foolish.

Φρόνησις • Philosophical Insights

Through the lens of classical Greek philosophy, discover how each character's hamartia serves as both their greatest strength and ultimate downfall. Our analysis reveals the duality of human nature—how the very traits that elevate heroes to greatness often contain the seeds of their destruction, as foretold by the ancient tragedians.
